Shelter for a family

Shelter becomes another emergency during a flood condition. As the government flood shelter is not sufficient to provide shelters to all affected people we also provide shelter to them with your donation.

Education for a Child

Education could be seriously harmed by the flood. As, most of the households drowned, children lost their books and other study material in the flood. We provide those materials when the flood situation gets better and students start their schools.

Cash donation is also crucial

After the flood when people go back to their habitats from the flood shelter they face terrible condition. Most of their house and household goods are being destroyed by the water. At that time food and other goods relief are not enough to fight back and restart their life again. They need cash.
